This is YouSearch

Easily find what you want within YouTube Videos


Fast, reliable, accurate

Whether it is looking for a specific item in a review, to a specific topic in a school lecture, to a specific part in your favorite podcast, YouSearch promises to find exactly what you are looking for in any YouTube video.

Built entirely on AWS

The speed, reliability, and accuracy of YouSearch relies entirely on AWS. YouSearch initially pulls your YouTube video into a secure Amazon S3 Bucket for analysis. It then proceeds to utilize cutting-edge speech recognition and natural language processing techniques through Amazon Transcribe for the transcription process of the video and Amazon Comprehend for sentiment analysis.
